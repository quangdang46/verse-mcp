# Industrial Galleries
Use Industrial Gallery pieces to create warehouse and factory structures.
![Industrial Galleries](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/4edba799-e4ce-43cc-8adc-e46d52c8a774?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
**Industrial** Galleries share similar [themes](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#island-theme) of factory and warehouse structures and props.
In the **Galleries** [category](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#category), you can browse for building pieces to make unique structures. The Galleries tab also offers environmental and prop items as well.
This page is a visual guide to help you find gallery that fit this theme more quickly.
To learn more about how to place gallery pieces to create an island that’s uniquely your own, check out [Building Basics](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/building-basics-in-fortnite-creative).
