# Decoration Galleries
Use these galleries to decorate and fill empty spaces on your island.
![Decoration Galleries](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/43d8481e-4561-4662-8db3-5fd762661b6d?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
**Decoration** galleries have various themes and can be used as items to place as decoration on your island .
In the **Galleries** tab, you can browse for building pieces to make unique structures. The Galleries tab also offers environmental and prop items as well. You can search by [category](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#category), or by gallery name (shown below).
This page is a visual guide to help you find galleries that this theme more quickly.
To learn more about how to place gallery pieces to create an island that’s uniquely your own, check out [Building Basics](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/building-basics-in-fortnite-creative).
