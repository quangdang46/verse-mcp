# search and destroy
Search and destroy is a round-based game type in which a team of attackers pairs off against a team of defenders, swapping sides at the halfway point.
Search and destroy is a [round](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite-creative/fortnite-creative-glossary#round)-based [game type ](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite-creative/fortnite-creative-glossary#gametype)in which a team of attackers pairs off against a team of defenders, swapping sides at the halfway point.
