# Restorative Items
Regenerate damaged health and shields with these cool items.
![Restorative Items](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/26e68733-0885-43bd-a683-9ac7e4f8e026?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
Players can restore damaged [health](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#health) and [shield](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#shield) with **Restorative** [items](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary).
Offer these items in combat gameplays where players could lose health while battling other players or [AIs](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#ai).
You can provide these items through devices that can [spawn](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#spawning) or [grant](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#grant) items like the [**Item Spawner**](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-item-spawner-devices-in-fortnite-creative).
Restorative items include:
  * Slurp Mushroom
  * Shield Mushroom
  * Pizza Slice
  * Pizza Party
  * Guzzle Juice
  * Slurp Juice

##  Finding and Placing Items
[![Finding Restorative Consumables](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/eadca8bc-8894-451c-b9b4-9a3552d35fa1?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/eadca8bc-8894-451c-b9b4-9a3552d35fa1?resizing_type=fit)
_Click image to enlarge._
  1. From [Create mode](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#create-mode), press the **Tab** key, then click **CREATIVE** on the [top navigation bar](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) to select the [CREATIVE inventory](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) screen if not already displayed.
  2. Click the **CONSUMABLES** tab.
  3. Scroll to find and select the item, use the **Search** box to look up the item by name, or check the list of relevant **Categories** specific to the item you're looking for to filter the view.
  4. Click the item, then click either **Equip** or **Add To Chest**.

Clicking **Equip** will add the item to your [Equipment bar](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary). When you're back in Create mode, you can view any items you've [equipped](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) by pressing the **F** key. You can select equipped items by either scrolling your middle mouse button or by pressing its corresponding number on your keyboard.
From the [Chest](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) tab, you can select either **Create Chest** or **Create Llama** to store the items in a [chest](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#chest) or a [llama](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#llama) for use [in-game](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#in-game).
[![Restorative Items Chest](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/c621b1d0-84ba-452a-8adc-6e4139d7db9d?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/c621b1d0-84ba-452a-8adc-6e4139d7db9d?resizing_type=fit)
_Click image to enlarge._
Chests and llamas are a great way to offer item bundles to players. Selecting **Add To Chest** will add the item to the **Chest** tab. Each time you click **Add To Chest** , the item count will increase by one, shown as a yellow box on the Chest tab.
You can add up to fifteen items to the **Chest** tab. When it’s full, the **Add To Chest** button will disappear. To add more items, you first have to remove items from the **Chest** tab.
There are many ways to offer these items to players. Use any of the devices under the [Registering Items](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-restorative-items-in-fortnite-creative#registering-items) section to either offer or require these items during gameplay.
##  Managing Items
[![Restorative Loadout Bar](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/02588cfc-3f82-49b9-8b54-a34a38251fd4?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/02588cfc-3f82-49b9-8b54-a34a38251fd4?resizing_type=fit)
You can manage these items when you are in [Play inventory](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#play-inventory). To access the **Play** inventory screen, press **Tab** and click **Play** in the top navigation bar. Here, you can swap item positions or drop them from your Equipment bar.
You cannot reposition or copy items with the phone tool. To delete an item from your inventory in Create mode, you will have to select either _Respawn_ or _Back To Hub_ from the _Menu_. When you do this, your inventory clears.
##  Using Restorative Items
Use these items to replenish damaged health and shield. It takes half a second to consume these items.
|  Item  |  Usage
---|---|---
[![Slurp Mushroom](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/af1e01c5-a32c-4e72-92b7-84649dd1e6bb?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/af1e01c5-a32c-4e72-92b7-84649dd1e6bb?resizing_type=fit) |  **Slurp Mushroom** |  Restores damaged health by 10 points. If health is full, any remaining points will go towards shield. Players carry up to 15 of this item in a stack.
[![Shield Mushroom](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/47b72d45-3530-41e7-80da-54779ef539b0?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/47b72d45-3530-41e7-80da-54779ef539b0?resizing_type=fit) |  **Shield Mushroom** |  Restores damaged shield by five points. Players can carry up to 15 of this item in a stack.
[![Pizza Slice](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/5ecb59ae-ce14-4be7-bd8f-06dd95a99511?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/5ecb59ae-ce14-4be7-bd8f-06dd95a99511?resizing_type=fit) |  **Pizza Slice** |  Simultaneously restores 25 points of damaged health and shield. Players can carry up to three of this item in a stack.
[![Pizza Party](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/6759c01a-2015-4f9a-8ec5-9d75c8039965?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/6759c01a-2015-4f9a-8ec5-9d75c8039965?resizing_type=fit) |  **Pizza Party** |  This item includes eight **Pizza Slices** that can either be equipped or consumed. Players can carry only one of this item in a stack.
[![Guzzle Juice](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/53743eb0-615a-49de-924b-dc51bedb8317?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/53743eb0-615a-49de-924b-dc51bedb8317?resizing_type=fit) |  **Guzzle Juice** |  Slowly restores damaged health up to 100. Healing will stop if damage is taken while this effect is active. Players can carry up to two of this item in a stack.

If health is full, any remaining points will go towards shield.
##  Registering Items
[![Registered Consumables](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/39884332-3e69-4107-a5ca-09a08c8d7b93?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/39884332-3e69-4107-a5ca-09a08c8d7b93?resizing_type=fit)
You can drop items directly onto devices that can either hold or [grant](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#grant) items. You must [register](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#register) these items to save in the device's memory.
[![Registering Consumables](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/3e621369-4a65-4cf7-8bd5-bb15769e9b18?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/3e621369-4a65-4cf7-8bd5-bb15769e9b18?resizing_type=fit)
_To register items to a device, stand directly on or immediately beside the device._
To register an item for this kind of device, follow the steps below. (You can also watch a [video tutorial](https://mediaspace.unrealengine.com/media/RegisteringCraftingConsumablesinFortniteCreative/1_zpmj3v0g) that shows you how to register items, for [crafting](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#crafting) or other [in-game](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#in-game) use.)
  1. In the CREATIVE inventory, find the equipment and items you want to register with a device and equip them.
  2. In Create mode, stand directly beside the device that will register the item.
  3. Press the **Tab** key to open the **PLAY** inventory screen.
  4. Click the item, then press either **Z** or **X** to split or drop the item. You can also drag the item to the side until a [backpack icon](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#backpack-icon) appears.

The compatible device will automatically register the dropped item. Compatible devices that can hold items include:
  * **Vending Machine**
  * **Team Settings & Inventory**
  * **Class Designer**
  * **Capture Item Spawner**
  * **Capture Area**
  * **Item Granter**
  * **Item Spawner**
  * **Conditional Button**
  * **Elimination Manager**
  * **Item Remover**

Use these devices to set up your own system for granting and spawning items onto your island.
##  Using Items for Crafting
You can pair items with each other and with weapons to create the process of [crafting](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#crafting), where players exchange one item or group of items for another.
To do this, choose from the list of compatible devices above like the [**Conditional Button**](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-conditional-button-devices-in-fortnite-creative) and [**Item Spawner**](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-item-spawner-devices-in-fortnite-creative) to set up the crafting process.
For example, you can require [](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-nature-items-in-fortnite-creative)**[Bacon](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-wild-edible-crafting-items-in-fortnite-creative)** and [](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-wild-edible-crafting-items-in-fortnite-creative)**[Blue Mushrooms](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-nature-items-in-fortnite-creative)** as items needed to craft **Pizza Slice**.
Visit our [video tutorials](https://mediaspace.unrealengine.com/playlist/dedicated/208434573/1_gxu6mwv5/1_qfnz9w5c) to learn more about working with items and for tips to enhance gameplay.
