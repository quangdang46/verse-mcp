# Template Islands
Learn about the template islands available, and how to use them to design and build your own island experience! 
![Template Islands](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/8eeaf5f5-1525-4a62-b888-7962f15a4a98?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
On this page
A **template island** helps you get started with a specific design concept or [game mode](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#game-mode), such as a [zone war](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#zone-war), [box fight](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#box-fight), or [escape](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#escape-game) island.
Each [template island](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#template-island) gives you the opportunity to review the [game mechanics](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#game-mechanics) and [devices](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#device) used to create them. You can also alter the island to fit with your own design.
##  How to Use Template Documentation 
Some templates have [tutorials](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#tutorial) to go with them, providing more details and information about the template construction and how the island experience was put together. You can use these tutorials with the island templates for a more in-depth view, or as a guide to creating your own island experience.
##  Accessing Template Islands 
  1. From the [golden rift](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#golden-rift), select **Use Console** to change your island destination.
[![standing in front of the golden rift console](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/9a628be2-c89f-456e-b956-5018cccbdf69?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/9a628be2-c89f-456e-b956-5018cccbdf69?resizing_type=fit)
  2. Click **+ Create New** from the **Game Creation** screen.
[![creating a new island](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/0504676b-6d67-455c-a244-e76d6f4010a8?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/0504676b-6d67-455c-a244-e76d6f4010a8?resizing_type=fit)
  3. On the **Select Type** screen, click the **Template Islands** tab. From here, you can review each template island available. Select the template you want, then click **Confirm**.
[![selecting a template](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/9626b2e4-c417-4a31-a084-a63193ef6da1?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/9626b2e4-c417-4a31-a084-a63193ef6da1?resizing_type=fit)
