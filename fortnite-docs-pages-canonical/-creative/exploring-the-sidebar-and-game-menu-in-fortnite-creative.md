# Exploring the Sidebar and Game Menu
The Sidebar is your access to the Fortnite Game Menu and many other options. 
![Exploring the Sidebar and Game Menu](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/baaa82f5-75b4-4d6d-b6e5-3acb9ff32387?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
On this page
The **sidebar** is the menu that pops out from the right when you press the **Esc** key. You can access the sidebar across all Fortnite [game genres](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#game-genre) (Save the World, Battle Royale, Creative and so on), although the choices listed in the sidebar may differ, depending on which genre you are in, and where in a game or [experience](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#experience) you are.
Press the **Esc key** to open the sidebar and you'll see that it includes many different sections.
  * Find your **Profile** options by clicking your character portrait and the top left of the panel.
  * The **Social** options are displayed when you click one of four icons at the top of the left navigation bar on the panel: **Party Up** , **Add Friends** , **Looking for Party** , and **Voice Chat**. By default, the panel opens with the **Party Up** options displayed.
  * Open the **Game Menu** by clicking the **gear** (**Menu**) icon above the **red arrow** (**Exit**) icon.
  * Return to the lobby or close Fortnite by clicking the **red arrow** (**Exit**) icon.


Each of the sidebar icons are explained in more detail in the following sections below.

##  Settings 
[![Settings menu bar](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/a5dfbc56-d285-4895-8513-31280e016994?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/a5dfbc56-d285-4895-8513-31280e016994?resizing_type=fit)
_Click image to enlarge._
Selecting the **Settings** option from the sidebar menu opens a new page. This is where you can find the **User Settings**. The User Settings allow you to change and customize a number of settings to tailor your game experience to your needs. The menu bar at the top of the settings page has 10 different settings.
  1. **Video** : Determines your display and graphics settings.
  2. **Volume** : Change the volume on different parts of the Fortnite experience. You can also set the sound quality, voice chat settings, and whether to play licensed audio or not.
  3. **Game** : Set your language, movement, combat style, building, and tutorial tips to enhance your gaming experience. You can even determine your extra game options, replays, and energy saving settings.
  4. **Game UI** : Determine what displays on your HUD and the reticle and damage feedback you receive.
  5. **Touch and Motion:** Change the gyro options to further customize your gaming experience.
  6. **Mouse & Keyboard**: Set your mouse sensitivity and keyboard movement to determine how your player moves. Mouse Flight and Tank Settings determine how your character pilots airplanes and tanks in-game.
  7. **Keyboard Controls** : Create presets for keybinding to the mouse or keyboard. Change which keys control the character movement in-game.
  8. **Controller** : Determine how your character moves, draws a weapon, as well as the controller deadzone, and sensitivity of the controller.
  9. **Controller Mapping** : Create controller binding presets based on different preset options. Set your controller platform and which buttons control the character's movement, building, and other character options.
  10. **Account & Privacy**: Find your account information and social privacy settings as well as clipping, gameplay privacy, credits, and legal information.


##  The Game Menu 
The options on this menu change, depending on where you are in Fortnite.
The three areas where you can access this menu are:
  * [From the lobby](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/exploring-the-sidebar-and-game-menu-in-fortnite-creative)
  * [From the hub](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/exploring-the-sidebar-and-game-menu-in-fortnite-creative)
  * [From your island](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/exploring-the-sidebar-and-game-menu-in-fortnite-creative)


###  Game Menu While in the Lobby 
[![Game Menu Options When You Are in the Lobby](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/11ebcb4b-5c9f-4406-adcd-fabc7e45514f?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/11ebcb4b-5c9f-4406-adcd-fabc7e45514f?resizing_type=fit)
The **Game Menu** tab has the following options when you are in the Fortnite lobby:
  * **Fortnite Crew** : See what’s in the Fortnite Crew pack and purchase the crew pack from the sidebar.
  * **Parenting Controls** : If you are a minor, your parents can use this to set limits on your game experience.
  * **Reporting** : Here you can report problems or bugs, report violations of the Code of Conduct, and provide feedback to the [dev team](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary). You can also review the Community Rules.
  * **Support and Refunds** : When you click this option, you can either open the [Fortnite Support](https://www.epicgames.com/help/en-US/fortnite-c75) web page or return and cancel purchases.
  * **Legal** : When you click this option, it opens the **Fortnite Legal Information** dialog box. This has two tabs, **Legal Information** and **Credits**. Legal Information takes you to page where you can review the **EULA** (End User License Agreement), **Third Party Software** , **Privacy Policy** , **Fan Content Policy** , **Terms of Service** , and **Close**.


[![Fortnite Legal Information Dialog Box](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/c419b39d-f9a4-404f-b8f7-bc6cb0f0adf2?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/c419b39d-f9a4-404f-b8f7-bc6cb0f0adf2?resizing_type=fit)
  * **Credits** : Select this to see who has worked or is working on Fortnite or Unreal.
This is an extremely long list, as past employees and contractors do not get removed from the list when they leave Epic.
  * **Fortnite Media** : Provides a way for you to turn on and off Fortnite announcements and media in the sidebar.

##  Exit from the Lobby 
[![Exit Options When You Are in the Lobby](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/6cd01c42-ca3d-421d-95c2-225393c308bf?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/6cd01c42-ca3d-421d-95c2-225393c308bf?resizing_type=fit)
If you click the **Exit** tab while you are in lobby, or while playing a game, the options are **Leave Party** (this is disabled if you are not in a party), and **Close Fortnite**.
[![Close Fortnite Confirmation Dialog](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/67ee183f-c8b1-4aba-92bf-092b2e411954?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/67ee183f-c8b1-4aba-92bf-092b2e411954?resizing_type=fit)
When you click **Close Fortnite** a confirmation dialog opens, asking "Are you sure you want to exit Fortnite?". Click **Yes** to close the game, or **No** to close the Sidebar and return to where you were.
##  Exit from the Hub or Your Island 
[![Exit Options in the Hub or on Your Island](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/d5dfadf7-be8a-4cbe-b39a-24de711865f2?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/d5dfadf7-be8a-4cbe-b39a-24de711865f2?resizing_type=fit)
If you click the **Exit** icon when you are in the Hub, or on your island in Create mode, you can select **Return to Lobby** or **Close Fortnite**. If you are on your island and want to return to the Hub, open the Game Menu and click **Back to Hub**.
[![Return to Lobby Confirmation Dialog](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/94679a1b-f9e0-43d1-b580-894f1d291971?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/94679a1b-f9e0-43d1-b580-894f1d291971?resizing_type=fit)
If you click **Return to Lobby** a confirmation dialog opens, asking "Leave and return to the lobby?". Click **Yes** to return to the lobby, or **No** to return to the Hub or your island.
[![Close Fortnite Confirmation Dialog](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/a9bc6c93-29fc-4963-b1f0-ee49ba5ddcb8?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/a9bc6c93-29fc-4963-b1f0-ee49ba5ddcb8?resizing_type=fit)
If you click **Close Fortnite** a confirmation dialog opens, asking "Are you sure you want to exit Fortnite?". Click **Yes** to close the game, or **No** to close the Sidebar and return to where you were.
