# Chest and Ammo Gallery Devices
This gallery offers items that will aid players in combat gameplay. 
![Chest and Ammo Gallery Devices](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/2c594a3c-ecef-451e-aae8-81a64fc8f173?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
On this page
Use the **Chest and Ammo Gallery** to offer [items](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) to players that will aid in combat [gameplay](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary). Players can open [chests](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) to receive a random combination of weapons, ammo, health items, and world [resources](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) such as wood, stone, or metal.
Selecting this [gallery](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) will [place](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) a collection of chests of various types on your island, and you can decide which best suits your needs.
You can copy your preferred chest by activating your [phone](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) tool and left-clicking to drag a duplicate. Duplicated chests do not offer identical items. Every chest offers a random selection of items with different quantities. You can remove any unwanted chests as explained under [Moving and Removing the Device](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-chest-and-ammo-gallery-devices-in-fortnite-creative).
For information on how to find the Chest and Ammo Gallery in the Devices category, see [Using Devices](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-devices-in-fortnite).
##  Moving and Removing the Device 
To move a chest to a different location, aim at it with the phone and right-click to drag it where you want it.
To remove an unwanted Chest from the gallery, aim at the device with your phone and press **X**.
You can access equipped items from the chest by pressing the **Tab** key when you have your pickaxe equipped.
From there, you can drop items by right-clicking an item and dragging it to the side until a Backpack icon appears, or by clicking the item and pressing **X**.
Resources such as ammo and wood can be split, dropped into stacks, or dropped entirely. Dropping an item will store it on the ground, but will not delete the item.
You can leave the island boundary to clear the your inventory and delete all equipped items.
##  Chest Variations 
###  Ammo Case 
[![Ammo Case](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/72c9cf7b-4f08-4e30-b66f-c3752ff99e09?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/72c9cf7b-4f08-4e30-b66f-c3752ff99e09?resizing_type=fit)
The **Ammo Chest** offers a random selection of [Light Bullets](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) (SMGs, pistols), [Medium Bullets](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) (revolvers, rifles, pistols), [Shells](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) (shotguns), a **Med Kit** , a **Small Shield Potion** , and a **Shield Potion**.
###  Big Ammo Case 
[![Big Ammo Case](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/3bbffdf5-df75-42eb-85b3-185e41c3cee6?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/3bbffdf5-df75-42eb-85b3-185e41c3cee6?resizing_type=fit)
The **Big Ammo Case** offers a random selection of [World Resources](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary), [ammo](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary), a **Shield Potion** , **Bandages** , and a weapon of random quality with extra corresponding ammo.
###  Cooler 
[![Cooler](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/507bba4f-adff-4ef0-931d-912f8bc9342c?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/507bba4f-adff-4ef0-931d-912f8bc9342c?resizing_type=fit)
The **Cooler** offers a random selection of edible items such as peppers, apples and so on.
###  Fire Petal Themed Chest 
[![](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/01edcca2-8464-474c-81cb-26812cdc1ccc?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/01edcca2-8464-474c-81cb-26812cdc1ccc?resizing_type=fit)
The Fire Petal Themed Chest offers Oni inspired items such as the Oni Mask and Oni Shotgun or the Demon Mask and Typhoon Blade. Additional items offered from the chest include high tier weapons, ammunition, and consumable items. Different sprites can be incorporated to influence the look of the chest.
###  God Power Faction Chest 
[![God Power Faction Chest](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/9220ef0c-d1d8-4058-978f-68e5cda78f0c?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/9220ef0c-d1d8-4058-978f-68e5cda78f0c?resizing_type=fit)
The **God Power Faction** chest drops a random number of weapons and items related to the Olympus and Underworld factions, as well as regular health and shield items.
When placed, it has a 50% chance of using either the Olympus visuals or the Underworld visuals, with no differences in loot. The Olympus version is more gold with yellow glowing lines, and the Underworld version is more bronze with blue-tinted glowing lines. They also have different markers.
###  Heist Bag 
[![Heist Bag](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/b7428ab8-4159-4175-9906-fe92d55313d1?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/b7428ab8-4159-4175-9906-fe92d55313d1?resizing_type=fit)
The **Heist Bag** offers an Uncommon or Rare weapon (pistol, shotgun, SMG, or rifle); two items (these can be healing items, or other items like grenades or traps); and a heist tool (such as Rocket Ram or remote explosives).
###  Hero Chest 
[![](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/7167f81e-7bf3-42cf-8844-8d086affbb86?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/7167f81e-7bf3-42cf-8844-8d086affbb86?resizing_type=fit)
The Hero Chest has unique features that other chests don't. You can assign the chest to a rank level and create custom messaging for the chest to inform players whether they can access the chest or not. The chest uses a beacon to alert players to its presence in-game.
The Hero Chest contains a random selection of items such as assault rifles, medium ammo, and medium shield potion. 
###  Oathbound Chest 
[![Oathbound Chest](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/e10efd3a-185e-471f-b559-04f056942e5b?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/e10efd3a-185e-471f-b559-04f056942e5b?resizing_type=fit)
The Oathbound Chest offers a random selection of [World Resources](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary); a specialty weapon such as the **Shockwave Hammer** , **Guardian Shield** or **Falcon Scout** ; a **Shield Potion** ; one to two random healing items; and a weapon of random quality with extra corresponding ammo. It is also possible to randomly receive a throwable object, such as a Grenade.
###  Produce Box 
[![Produce Box](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/5407bb20-6cd9-4fb2-9532-7fbbf947e6d6?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/5407bb20-6cd9-4fb2-9532-7fbbf947e6d6?resizing_type=fit)
The **Produce Box** offers a random selection of edible items such as peppers, apples and so on.
###  Wooden Chest 
[![Wooden Chest](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/6b55ca6c-5075-48e3-b694-b3f71a292d7a?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/6b55ca6c-5075-48e3-b694-b3f71a292d7a?resizing_type=fit)
The **Wooden Chest** offers a random selection of World Resources; **Bandages** ; a **Grenade** or other throwable item; **Small Shield Potions** ; and an [Uncommon](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) weapon with extra corresponding ammo.
###  Treasure Chest 
[![Treasure Chest](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/46a445f1-f6c8-437f-b74d-c8173c008ba5?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/46a445f1-f6c8-437f-b74d-c8173c008ba5?resizing_type=fit)
The **Treasure Chest** offers World Resources; a [Launch Pad](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary); a **Shield Potion** ; a [Legendary or Epic weapon](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary) with extra corresponding ammo; and a **Small Shield Potion**.
