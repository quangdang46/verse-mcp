# Audio and Visual Effects Galleries
Use props that have audio and visual effects to give your island extra flair. 
![Audio and Visual Effects Galleries](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/85286ab0-b9fa-48cf-842b-63cb34a363c4?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
On this page
**Audio and Visual Effect** [galleries](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#gallery) share similar themes with props that have auditory or visual effects for use in gameplay.
On the [Galleries tab](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary), you can browse for building pieces to make unique structures. The Galleries tab also offers environmental and prop items. You can search by [category](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#category), or by gallery name (shown below).
This page is a visual guide to help you find gallery items that fit this theme more quickly.
To learn more about how to place gallery pieces to create an island that’s uniquely your own, check out [Building Basics](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/building-basics-in-fortnite-creative).
