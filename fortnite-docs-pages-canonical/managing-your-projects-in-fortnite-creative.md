# Managing Your Projects
Learn how to manage your island projects in Creator Portal.
![Managing Your Projects](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/d579c140-eb81-48aa-ab39-58d3f3e6d40e?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
Learn how to manage your projects using Creator Portal, including an explanation of the project navigation menu, and descriptions of the analytics page for your project, the page showing any in-game offers in your project, and the Verse runtime errors page. You can also learn about sharing links, and A/B testing for your islands' thumbnail images.
