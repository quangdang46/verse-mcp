# Importing Custom Audio
Import custom audio into your island and immerse players in the world of your creation.
![Importing Custom Audio](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/6940c479-1213-43e2-94a0-12fa1f74bac1?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
Import **audio files** into your projects in **Unreal Editor for Fortnite (UEFN)** by dragging your audio files directly from your computer into the Content Browser.
By uploading content, you confirm that it does not infringe or violate someone else's rights, including their intellectual property rights (such as by infringing another’s copyright or trademark), and that you own or have obtained the necessary rights to share the content.
Your imported audio is also expected to follow our [Fortnite Developer Rules](https://www.fortnite.com/news/fortnite-island-creator-rules).
Refer to the [UEFN Supplemental Terms](https://www.fortnite.com/unreal-editor-for-fortnite-supplemental-terms) for more information.
##  Supported Formats
UEFN supports the following audio formats:
  * .wav
  * .aif
  * .flac
  * .ogg

##  Alternate Methods
You can also import audio into UEFN by:
  * Clicking **Import** in the **Content Browser** , then selecting the file from your hard drive.
  * Right-clicking in the **Content Browser** and selecting **Import** from the dropdown menu, then selecting a file from your hard drive.
