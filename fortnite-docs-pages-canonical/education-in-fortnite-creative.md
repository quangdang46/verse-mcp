# Education
Find lesson plans that are designed for use by educators in the classroom. 
![Education](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/e09ac388-a8b3-4e6a-be67-c521eeb71076?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
On this page
Free Fortnite lesson plans are available to educators who want to use Creative or Unreal Editor for Fortnite (UEFN) in creative ways in the classroom.
Fortnite, through the Creative toolset and UEFN, brings a whole new dimension to learning with interactivity that can be controlled by your students, and it's a great way to engage their interest.
These lesson plans cover a range of subjects, including history, geography and programming, and address a variety of skill sets, from writing and graphic design to puzzle solving and city planning. For more available lesson plans using UEFN, check out [Education in Unreal Editor for Fortnite](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/uefn/education-in-unreal-editor-for-fortnite).
Are you an educator who wants to create your own lesson plan? Download our [free lesson plan template](https://cdn2.unrealengine.com/lesson-plan-template-089033580639-bb1dade4fcbb.pdf) and have some fun!
##  What Is the Educational Value of Fortnite Creative? 
Fortnite Creative and Unreal Editor for Fortnite are [sandbox environments](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#sandbox-game). These toolsets can be used to develop a variety of immersive interactive experiences. This includes games _and_ educational and historical experiences such as the [TIME Studios March Through Time ](https://www.epicgames.com/fortnite/en-US/creative/docs/march-through-time-experience-in-fortnite-creative) experience.
On the underlying foundation of [Unreal Engine](https://www.unrealengine.com/en-US), students can take advantage of thousands of beautiful Fortnite assets to build experiences for areas and ideas that interest them. Studies show the educational value of games in learning and engagement, and Fortnite in the classroom is no exception.
By working with Fortnite, students have an opportunity to gain insights into game technology and design, develop new skills that can port into many areas of life, and to use the space to creatively employ their ideas and imaginations in building epic experiences — in every sense!
