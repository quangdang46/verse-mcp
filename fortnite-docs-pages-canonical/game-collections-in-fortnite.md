On this page
Fortnite brings unparalleled value by partnering with top brands to offer you exclusive assets and mechanics for your islands. These collaborations provide access to unique characters, environments, and gameplay elements from some of the world's most popular franchises. Whether it’s designing an action-packed TMNT battle arena, a terrifying The Walking Dead Universe survival map, or crafting a story-driven LEGO® adventure, the possibilities are endless.
Through these partnerships, you gain access to high-quality assets that capture the essence of each brand, from custom prefabs and galleries to playable templates. These exclusive resources provide the opportunity for you to build more immersive and recognizable worlds, enhancing gameplay and visual appeal. You can engage players on a new level by leveraging familiar characters, themes, and environments. Islands built with these assets are known as brand islands.
