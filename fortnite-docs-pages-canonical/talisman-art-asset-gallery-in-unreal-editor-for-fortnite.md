# Talisman Art Asset Gallery
Browse through the Talisman Environment's custom assets to use for your own projects.
![Talisman Art Asset Gallery](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/e5e68342-2b5c-4eb5-be2d-5253d8bb709a?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
The [**Talisman: Environment**](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/talisman-environment-template-in-unreal-editor-for-fortnite) template showcases an out-of-this-world environment created using custom assets. These assets are packaged and arranged in a new level within the template for you to browse and use for your own projects.
[![Content Browser](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/15dd0ab9-fc6e-4b50-ba0c-167df43c4cfe?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/15dd0ab9-fc6e-4b50-ba0c-167df43c4cfe?resizing_type=fit)
To access these assets within the Talisman: Environment template, navigate to your project's folder within the **Content Browser** and select **AssetArtGallery**.
[![](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/c54c9089-098c-41b7-8f0b-312c38fe0ea9?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/c54c9089-098c-41b7-8f0b-312c38fe0ea9?resizing_type=fit)
In the top toolbar, you can also navigate to **File** > **Open Level** to open the **AssetArtGallery** level.
Talisman: Environment's assets are thematically sorted into the following categories.
  * [Building](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/talisman-art-asset-gallery-in-unreal-editor-for-fortnite#building)
  * [Props](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/talisman-art-asset-gallery-in-unreal-editor-for-fortnite#props)
  * [Structures and Kits](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/talisman-art-asset-gallery-in-unreal-editor-for-fortnite)
  * [Materials and Lighting](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/talisman-art-asset-gallery-in-unreal-editor-for-fortnite)
