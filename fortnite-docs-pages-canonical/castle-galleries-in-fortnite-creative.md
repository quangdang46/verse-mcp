# Castle Galleries
Become the ruler of your world by using castle galleries.
![Castle Galleries](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/d794e76e-b7a1-4387-a922-8459dee88130?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
**Castle** [galleries](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#gallery) share [themes](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#island-theme) of fortress and mansion structures and props.
In the **Galleries** [category](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#category), you can browse for building pieces to make unique structures. The Galleries tab also offers environmental and prop items.
This page is a visual guide to help you find galleries that fit this theme more quickly.
To learn more about how to place gallery pieces to create an island that's uniquely your own, check out [Building Basics](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/building-basics-in-fortnite-creative).
