# Materials
Learn how to create materials and textures to make your island more visually engaging. 
![Materials](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/00f1eeeb-6c46-48e3-a83d-88a34938675a?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
Creating your own [materials](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/unreal-editor-for-fortnite-glossary#material) and [textures](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/unreal-editor-for-fortnite-glossary#texture) in **Unreal Editor for Fortnite (UEFN)** gives the [meshes](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/unreal-editor-for-fortnite-glossary#mesh) you use on your island a unique look and feel.
Explore the pages on material properties to see tutorials on how to create or customize materials and textures, the most used material nodes, and more.
