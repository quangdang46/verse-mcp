# Video Tutorials
These videos will take you from zero to sixty on how to build island experiences in Fortnite Creative.
![Video Tutorials](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/6dad973f-8f47-41d7-bc51-1aa03a414f3a?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
Want to see how grid snapping works instead of reading about it? Or learn some great tips and tricks on working with items? Check out the [FN Create YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@FortniteCreate/videos) these and so much more! Whatever your preferred style of learning is, we’re working to keep you in the know for all things Creative!
There are videos on a variety of topics, from intros to Creative to how to transition from Minecraft to Fortnite. You can also use these videos to follow along in our online lesson plans for educators and students!
##  Fortnite Creative 101
  1. [](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7o7AvUuoDw8)**[Get Started as a Fortnite Developer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7o7AvUuoDw8):** Find out more about Fortnite Creative and how to get started building your own games. The world can't wait to see what you create!
  2. [**Using the Fortnite Creative Toolset**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0wceTxlUAJ4): Learn the basics for the Fortnite Creative toolset and how to access it. Get tips on navigation, using hot keys, and using premade inventory from Fortnite. See how easy it is to get started!
  3. [**Tips and Tricks for the Fortnite Creative Toolset**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zwYLRkBa6zo): Find out how grid snapping can speed up your game building, learn to resize a prop or change its orientation, and get tips on other ways to manipulate props.
  4. [**Creative Items - What They Are and How To Use Them**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jS7wSLWDno0): Learn how to use items to craft other items in Fortnite, and how to customize devices to use them in gameplay.
  5. [**Onboarding Players To Your Fortnite Island**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bX3u3UR2jkE): Onboarding is how you show new players how to play your game. Learn some best practices when developing player onboarding.

##  Promoting Your Island
  1. [**How To Promote Your Fortnite Island**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W28qIx_zXvQ): This is where the real fun begins as you invite the world to visit your island! There are all kinds of cool ways you can promote your creation. Watch this video for a quick intro!
  2. [**Creating Promotional Videos For Your Fortnite Island**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ipdZgo8LeuE): Check out these tips on how to create great visuals for promoting your island on social media and the Discover UI.
  3. [**Using Replays**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mWhtgFDDLIA): Replays is a tool that packs a lot of potential when it comes to your social media game. You call the shots (literally!) when you use Replays in your games. Whether you're a content creator, an Fortnite developer, or a player who wants to boost your gameplay by studying your own games, Replays is the way to get an immersive view of recent games you've played.
  4. [**Fortnite Content Rules And Guidelines**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Jv_t48CjTE): Check out our overview of Fortnite’s content rules and guidelines, which help ensure that Fortnite remains a safe and welcoming environment for everyone.

##  Making Games
Different game modes use different game mechanics. See the basics of what goes into making some of these classic types of games
  1. [**Using The Creative Toolset For A 1v1 Game**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1dK6Y8OmLtg): Learn what makes up a 1v1 (player vs. player) game and how to build your own.
  2. [**Using The Creative Toolset For A Zone Wars Game**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wJJaIeY29Uc): Create your own Battle Royale game, including which devices to use to control stormsbehavior.
  3. [**Using The Creative Toolset For A Skillrun Game**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eIVrBWwDVko): Create a maze that one or more players can use to show their skills in evading traps and scaling obstacles.
  4. [**Using The Creative Toolset For A Box Fight Game**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VU3SPBApOKk): Learn the basic elements you'll need to build a box fight.
  5. [**Using The Creative Toolset For A Search-And-Destroy Game**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3UC5rzfASGM): This team-based game pits players against players. The video will take you from how to set up the island to how to create different ways of combat.
