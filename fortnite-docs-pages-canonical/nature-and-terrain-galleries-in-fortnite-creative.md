# Nature and Terrain Galleries
Use Nature and Terrain galleries to decorate your island. 
![Nature and Terrain Galleries](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/b4d29387-635e-4705-9d37-14c4adfd9263?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
On this page
**Nature and Terrain** galleries share similar themes of things found in nature, such as rock formations, foliage, and tree props.
On the **Galleries** tab, you can browse for building pieces to make unique structures. The Galleries tab also offers environmental and prop items as well. You can search by [category](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#category), or by gallery name (shown below).
This page is a visual guide to help you find galleries that fit this theme more quickly.
To learn more about how to place gallery pieces to create an island that’s uniquely your own, check out [Building Basics](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/building-basics-in-fortnite-creative).
