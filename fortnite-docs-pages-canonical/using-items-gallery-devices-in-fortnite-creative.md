# Items Gallery Devices
Use the Items Gallery to offer players a quick status buff.
![Items Gallery Devices](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/1cd7e141-686a-4526-bb45-35779fb26a80?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
You can use the **Items Gallery** to offer players a quick status buff during gameplay. These status buffs will temporarily give the consuming player an increase of either health, shield, speed boost, or jump boost.
Selecting the Items Gallery will place eight different items that you can choose from. The preferred item can be copied by equipping the cell phone device and left-clicking to drag a duplicate as explained in **[Moving and Removing the Device](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/using-items-gallery-devices-in-fortnite-creative)**.
Players can consume the item by standing near it and pressing **E**. Players will not be able to equip these items in their inventories.
Players can also stack items that offer status buffs, such as the **Hop Rock** and **Pepper**.
##  Finding and Placing the Device
[![](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/e139ac0e-302f-41e3-a3b5-6f7619fab654?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/e139ac0e-302f-41e3-a3b5-6f7619fab654?resizing_type=fit)
_Click image to enlarge._
  1. From [Create mode](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#create-mode), press the **Tab** key to open the [**Creative inventory**](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#creative%20inventory) window.
  2. Click the **Devices** tab and scroll to select the device. You can also use the **Search** box or the **Categories** panel on the left to find your device.
  3. Click **PLACE NOW** to [place](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#place) immediately, or put the device in the **[**QUICK BAR**](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#quick-bar)** to place later.
  4. Press **Esc** to return to your island in Create mode. Use your [phone](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/fortnite-creative-glossary#phone) to position the device, then left-click to place it. Press **P** to detach the device from your phone, then press **P** again to reactivate your phone.

##  Moving and Removing the Device
You can move items to a desired location by aiming at it with the cell phone equipped, then right-clicking to drag it to the desired position. You can remove any unwanted items from the gallery by aiming at the device with your phone and pressing **X**.
