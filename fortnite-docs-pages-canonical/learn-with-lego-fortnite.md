# Learn with LEGO® Fortnite
Find lessons that lean on LEGO Islands! 
![Learn with LEGO® Fortnite](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/aeb2b72b-30f9-4a37-9c8c-0e28afcc7bb1?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
On this page
Learning should be fun, and when a lesson takes place on a LEGO® Island, it is!
##  Available Lessons 
Ready to turn LEGO Fortnite into a educational platform? The lessons below do just that! Each one is a standalone lesson.
  * [**LEGO Fortnite Lesson Plan: Brick Farm**](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/learning/tutorials/kylO/lego-fortnite-lesson-plan-brick-farm) — The objective is to deepen students' understanding of sustainable farming practices, resource management, and the role of agriculture in community sustainability.
[![](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/1ebd1fe6-e801-4c99-8474-5854aef33acc?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/1ebd1fe6-e801-4c99-8474-5854aef33acc?resizing_type=fit)
  * [**Tackling Climate Change through LEGO Fortnite**](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/learning/tutorials/BJYR/tackling-climate-change-through-lego-fortnite) — Let these LEGO Minifigures show how we can live sustainably as we work to counter climate change.
[![](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/c2a22068-eb28-4d59-be5e-d44db541570b?resizing_type=fit)](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/c2a22068-eb28-4d59-be5e-d44db541570b?resizing_type=fit)
  * [**LEGO Fortnite - Inclusive Islands SDG Lesson Plan**](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/learning/tutorials/yjZa/lego-fortnite-inclusive-islands-sdg-lesson-plan) — Addressing how to make sure communities are accessible for citizens with disabilities and senior citizens is vital.
