# Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle Islands
All you need to know about building TMNT islands!
![Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle Islands](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/f56d65fb-4cfa-4f5d-98b0-43a1e0783c2a?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (TMNT) have arrived in Unreal Editor for Fortnite (UEFN), bringing with them an arsenal of exciting new tools, assets, and possibilities for developers. Whether you’re a seasoned developer or just starting, these new resources are designed to inspire and empower you to craft unique and immersive experiences.
Explore two new UEFN templates, designed to spark your imagination, and explore the TMNT Prefabs and Galleries!
With the arrival of TMNT in UEFN, a new world of creative possibilities awaits. Whether you’re building an epic battle against Shredder or crafting a unique adventure in the Turtles’ underground domain, these new tools and assets are designed to help you bring your ideas to life. Dive in, explore the resources, and let your creativity run wild as you craft the next great TMNT experience!
