# Campaigns
Increase your island's visibility in Discover with a Sponsored Row campaign. 
![Campaigns](https://dev.epicgames.com/community/api/documentation/image/adb36c52-3b84-4edf-a1fa-889358f6a608?resizing_type=fill&width=1920&height=335)
A **Sponsored Row Campaign** is a feature in Fortnite that you can use to increase the visibility of an island you feel isn’t performing as well as you believe it should. 
Campaigns are featured in the Sponsored Row, a clearly labeled row for promoted islands in Discover. 
You can create a campaign and manage it from the Creator Portal, where you measure their effectiveness.
